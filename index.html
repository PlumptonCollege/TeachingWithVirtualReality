Teaching with
Virtual Reality

www.teachingwithvirtualreality.com

In 2018, as part of the Education & Training Foundations' Outstanding Teaching Learning & Assessment grant programme, a partnership of Sussex further education providers in the United Kingdom explored the effective use of 360°, augmented and virtual reality technologies within the classroom.

The partnership will be publishing a series of case studies of teaching with virtual reality, an online best practice guide and final report here in July 2018
