Teaching with Virtual Reality

In 2018, as part of the Education & Training Foundations' Outstanding Teaching Learning & Assessment grant programme, a partnership of Sussex further education providers explored the effective use of 360°, augmented and virtual reality technologies within the classroom.

This website is an interactive guide for educators to learn
