Practical Virtual Reality

In 2018, as part of the Education & Training Foundations' Outstanding Teaching Learning & Assessment grant programme, a partnership of Sussex further education providers explored the effective use of 360°, augmented and virtual reality technologies within the classroom.

This website is an interactive guide for educators to learn how they can start experimenting with immersive technologies within their teaching.

Project Partners

The project would not have succeeded without the hard work of the students and staff at partner colleges, and the support of the project's funders, mentors and broadcasters:

Why Virtual Reality? Why now?

Emerging technologies like 360° photography, 360° video, augmented and virtual reality could have the potential to allow teachers to introduce immersive environments that enhance and accelerate their student’s learning outcomes.

Entry-level immersive technology has reached a price and complexity point where it is now accessible to the average classroom. But how can teachers effectively integrate these technologies within their lessons?
